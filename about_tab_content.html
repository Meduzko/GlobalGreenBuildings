VISION

When we consider that all buildings are inefficient, it is sensible to investigate how, where and why the inefficiencies occur. Identifying these flaws effectively results in knowing where the opportunities are.

Global Green Buildings believes that the roadmap to green real estate starts with advanced monitoring on the current systems, followed by the implementation of proven technologies that help reduce the waste caused by the existing installations.



MISSION

Global Green Buildings wants to accelerate the transition to more energy efficient real-estate.

Global Green Buildings

Founded in 2017 as a subsidiary of Global Green Investment Group, the company addresses the challenges of customers who want to become more energy efficient. With our 4-step approach we start a data-driven journey to green real estate that has more value for the owner, its occupants and the planet.
