albert heijn elst
Albert Heijn is the largest supermarket chain of the Netherlands. With almost a thousand stores their objective to reduce their overall footprint is high on the agenda. As front runners in terms of energy efficiency they continuously search for new solutions that benefit their buildings. The owner of Albert Heijn Elst was open to see what sort of solutions could optimise his building's performance. With our 4-step approach we have established a data driven roadmap that will reduce his energy consumption with approximately 26%.
Monitor
We start with our easy to implement intelligent monitoring system, that provides an accurate account of where and why energy is being consumed.

In consultation with our client we installed a patented Voltage Optimisation system that improves the quality of the supplied power with a 99.9% efficiency. The results of this solution can be found in reduced harmonics, lower phase imbalance and the optimal voltage for the equipments design.
